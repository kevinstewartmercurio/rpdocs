# Inviting Users

Once you log in as a coach or an admin you'll be able to invite new users to 
the portal. {{ className: 'lead' }}

Click the "Invite User" button and fill out the form. After you click "Invite",
the user will receive your invitation via email. {{ className: 'lead' }}
